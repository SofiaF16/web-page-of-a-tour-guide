Moscow

Everyone knows Red Square, the colourful domes of St. Basil's Cathedral, the ancient fortress of the Kremlin.
But! How much charm there is in walking the ancient streets of Moscow! From amazing temples on wide squares to hiding churches in narrow old alleys. From incredibly beautiful at any time of the year Novodevichy Monastery to wonderful so-called "Stalinist skyscrapers" or, as they are also called, "Seven Sisters". All this you can find in the magnificent city of Moscow.
Moscow is a city of constant movement. They say this is the city that never sleeps. You can feel its rhythm by going down to the beautiful underground palace - the Moscow subway or just walking the streets and looking at the faces of people running by. Also, you can go on a night tour of the city to see its exceptional beauty, which is unique at any time of the year.
I invite you to my hometown - Moscow, the only place that can help you to solve the great mystery, which is called "Russian soul". You will love this wonderful, unique, unlike anything else city. Megalopolis, which the Russians call the big village.
